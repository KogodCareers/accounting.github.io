CPA Eligibility Snapshot

Plan early: CPA exam and licensure eligibility are set by each state board of accountancy, not by the university or employer. Most CPA paths require a bachelor's degree, specific accounting/business coursework, passing the Uniform CPA Exam, professional experience, and often 150 total semester hours for licensure. Requirements can differ by jurisdiction, so students should compare their transcript against the state where they plan to apply and discuss timing with a Kogod advisor before recruiting for CPA-track roles.
